CyPlanner

Development - Android

CyPlanner was an Android application built for Computer Science 319 taught by Professor Basu. CyPlanner was intended to be a freshmans focal point during their college experience, including a dedicated class list, including a map of how to get to each class. Due to the loss of two team members the project only got as far as a list of classes, with the ability to add and remove, and locate them on a campus map.
